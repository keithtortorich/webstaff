FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

What's included in the free website? ▾
A fully designed, mobile-optimized, SEO-ready website built specifically for your trade. We handle design, copy, service pages, schema markup, and launch. You just provide your business details via our intake form.
How does the AI Receptionist actually work? ▾
When someone calls your Twilio number and you don't pick up, the AI Receptionist plays a brief message and immediately sends a personalized SMS to the caller — referencing your business by name and promising a fast callback. The lead is logged in your dashboard instantly. On Growth and Pro plans it responds within 60 seconds.
Does it work with ServiceTitan? ▾
Yes. Phase 1 integration is active now via a Zapier bridge — every new lead from your WebStaffr site automatically creates a customer and job in ServiceTitan. Native bidirectional API integration launches in weeks 4–8. We also support Jobber and Housecall Pro on the same timeline.
Do I need any technical skills? ▾
None. We set everything up for you. You complete the intake form, we build the site and activate your AI staff, and from that point everything runs automatically. Your dashboard shows you what the agents are doing in real time.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel with 30 days' notice. The website we built for you remains yours.
What trades do you serve? ▾
HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, general contracting, restaurants, med spas, dental practices, salons, and more. The site builder adapts typography, color, and content to each industry automatically.
How is this different from Broadly, Podium, or other tools? ▾
Those are tools. WebStaffr is a workforce. Instead of giving you software to manage, we give you named AI employees who handle specific jobs. The website is free — you're hiring staff, not buying a subscription to another dashboard.
